How to Play
Aim & Shoot

Drag from the ball in any direction. The further you pull, the harder the shot. Release to hit.

Zoom & Pan

Pinch to zoom in and out on mobile. On desktop, use scroll wheel. Drag the course to pan around.

Scoring

Each hole has a par. Fewer strokes is better. Complete all 9 holes to post your score to the leaderboard.

Multiplayer

Host a game and share the 4-letter code with friends. Take turns putting. Chat with the message button.
